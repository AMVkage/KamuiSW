Welcome to the Synestia development log/wiki!

The Start
In 2019, I started learning unity. I searched up tones of tutorials back then, and used stuff i found on the internet.
I slowly got better with programming and made alot of prototypes, but never actually finished any of those. Now after 4 years,
i think i could make a full unity game without searching up tutorials, so thats what i did. i started creating a project called "Synestia", and started working on it.
If you played the Breakthrough Minecraft mod, you will find a lot of familiar enemies and structures.
that is because I work together with the developer of the Breakthrough mod. We are close friends in real life,
and we just thought it would be cool if we connected our universes together.
